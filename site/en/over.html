Who is Peter / —

No agency.
One craftsman.

I'm Peter Stolk — an independent web designer from Uithoorn. For more than 7 years I've designed and built professional websites for entrepreneurs across the Netherlands. I work with HTML, CSS and GSAP for fully handcrafted sites, and with WordPress whenever that's the smartest choice for your project.

What sets me apart? You always deal directly with me — the person who actually builds your website. No account managers, no juniors, no offshore production. Just Peter, who understands what you need and delivers it.

I work with a select group of clients at a time. That way every project gets the attention it deserves — and you get the service you should be able to expect.

+Personal contact — always directly with Peter.
+Custom designs — no off-the-shelf templates.
+Responsive on every device — always tested.
+I'll say no if a project isn't the right fit.
+Hosting and maintenance handled too — if you want.
