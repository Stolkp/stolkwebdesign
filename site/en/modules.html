Frequently asked questions / —

Honest answers,
no marketing talk.

Do I always need the Base CMS?+
The Base CMS (€149 setup + €19/mo) is the foundation: your own login, dashboard, hosting and updates. You activate it once. After that you click modules on — Content, Invoicing, Social, Blog — each with its own small monthly rate, without paying for the base again. Only the SEO report can also be ordered separately, without the Base CMS.
Does this work without a Stolkwebdesign website?+
Eventually, yes. Right now the modules are designed for websites built by Stolkwebdesign, because the embed route and the design system are baked in by default. From Q3 2026 there will be a version for existing sites — join the waitlist to be the first to hear when.
When can I start?+
Base CMS, Content, Invoicing, Social and SEO are available to early-access clients from summer 2026. Blog follows in Q3 2026. Waitlist sign-ups get priority and keep the early-access rate, even after the official launch.
What if I want to stop?+
You pay the setup fee once; after that a flat monthly rate per active module. Cancel monthly — stop and the monthly rate stops too, no notice period or penalty. You can always export your content and invoice data.
Can I use my own domain name?+
Yes, always. The CMS runs on yoursite.nl/cms, your blog on yoursite.nl/blog. No Stolkwebdesign subdomain, no "powered by" bar.
Do I get a discount for multiple modules?+
For early-access clients who activate several modules at once there's a bundle rate. Book a short call with Peter — he'll work it out for you on the spot.
